About RadTexas/ Texas Radiation Online How Much Traffic Does This Site Get?
Texas Radiation Online has had a very productive first few months. Although only formally announced in January, by March it already was serving requests for 8000 hits and over 250MB of data per month, a good deal of public curiosity just in time for the radioactive waste "portion" of the 78th Texas State Legislature. Soon after, the number of monthly hits increased by over 3 times, and by October 3rd, it had served nearly 3 gigabytes of downloaded data. Below is a chart with the details, which will be updated on a regular basis (approx. monthly).

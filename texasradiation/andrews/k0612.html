1. WCS certifies it is licensed to possess, treat or transfer cesium-137 contaminated incident-related material.

2. WCS certifies the emission control dust and other incident-related material have been stored and transferred in accordance with operating procedures approved by the agency. A copy of our license is attached.

3. WCS certifies the total cesium-137 activity contained in emission control dust and other incident-related materials to be transferred to a hazardous waste disposal facility will not be transferred until specifically approved by the TDH and will provide a copy of that approval when received. WCS certifies the CS-137 activity will not exceed the total activity associated with the inadvertent melting incidents.

4. A copy of the notification to the hazardous waste disposal facility of the impending transfer of the incident related materials and a copy of the written agreement from the hazardous waste disposal facility to receive and dispose of the packaged or unpackaged material is attached.

5. If the stabilized material has been packaged, WCS certifies the packaged stabilized material will be packaged for transportation and disposal in non-bulk steel packaging as defined in the United States Department of Transportation regulations, Title 49 of the Code of Federal Regulations Section 173.213.

6. WCS certifies the stabilized material that will be packaged for transportation and disposal, at a hazardous waste disposal facility, in non-bulk steel packaging, as defined in the United States Department of Transportation regulations, Title 49 of the Code of Federal Regulations Section 173.213, will not contain pretreatment average concentrations of cesium-137 that exceed 130 picocuries per gram of material, above background.

7. WCS certifies the dose rate at 3.28 feet (1 meter) from the surface of any package containing stabilized waste for disposal at a hazardous waste disposal facility will not exceed 20 µrem per hour or 0.20 µSv per hour, above background.

8. WCS certifies that unpackaged stabilized material for to be disposed at a hazardous waste disposal facility will not contain pretreatment average concentrations of cesium-137 that exceed 100 picocuries per gram of material, above background.

9. WCS certifies the emission control dust and other material from electric arc furnaces or foundries to be disposed at a hazardous waste disposal facility will not contain other radionuclides that are not covered under an exemption.
